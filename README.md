## üöÄ Key Features

* **100% Offline Database:** All your business data is stored locally on your device for maximum privacy and speed.
* **Billing & Stock Management:** Efficiently process "Stock In" (Purchases) and "Stock Out" (Sales/Invoices).
* **Professional Reports:** Generate PDF and CSV reports for current inventory levels and total asset valuation.
* **Low Stock Alerts:** Visual warnings and alerts when items fall below your custom threshold.
* **Data Portability:** Complete Backup and Restore functionality to move your data between devices.
